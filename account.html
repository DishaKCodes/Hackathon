Scholarships

College of Engineering, Pune believes in promoting excellence in technical education with a desire to empower its students with high quality education and resources. It believes that no student irrespective of his challenges should be deprived of such education.

Various Government, Non-government and Industry/Alumni aided scholarships and financial aids are provided to the students. These scholarships are Private as well as Govt, and a few are exclusively for COEPians. Also, there are various scholarships exclusively for female students.
